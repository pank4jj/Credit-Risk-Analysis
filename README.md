# Credit Risk Analysis — Lending Club (2007–2018)
# Dataset - https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/wordsforthewise/lending-club

Analyzing 2.2M+ Lending Club loans to find what drives borrower default, quantify
default rates, and segment the portfolio into Low / Medium / High risk tiers —
delivered as an interactive Power BI dashboard.

**Skills shown:** Python (pandas), SQL, Statistics, Power BI, data cleaning, business reporting.

## The business question

> *Which borrowers are likely to default, and how do we group them by risk?*

A lender uses this to decide **who to approve, how to price loans, and which
accounts to monitor**.

## Results 

- Overall default rate: **19.96%**
- Grade A → G default rate: **9.04% → 49.93%**
- Riskiest loan purpose: **Small Business**
- High vs Low risk segment: **34.99% vs 12.42%**
